Who Am I?

“I love making things. I love learning things.”

I was hand-coding HTML back before CSS was popular, but left the field to explore all the options. After several years in print design, I’m back home with my text editor, wondering why I ever left.

I have a Bachelor of Arts degree in Classics from Connecticut College, which is surprisingly relevant to coding—and a graduate degree in public administration, which is less so. A nerd of all trades with competing interests in design and development. I’m currently taking the Freelance WordPress Developer Blueprint at Skillcrush, and loving every minute of it.

I have a background in the liberal arts and I am a voracious lifelong learner.
